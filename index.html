Biography

Leonardo da Vinci (1452-1519) was an Italian Renaissance polymath whose areas of interest included invention, painting, sculpting, architecture, science, music, mathematics, engineering, literature, anatomy, geology, astronomy, botany, writing, history, and cartography.

He is widely considered one of the greatest painters of all time and is credited with being the father of paleontology, ichnology, and architecture. His genius epitomized the Renaissance humanist ideal.
